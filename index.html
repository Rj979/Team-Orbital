Forging the Future of Motorsport

Team Orbital is the official go-kart racing team of the Rajadhani Institute of Engineering and Technology. We are a dedicated group of engineering students driven by a passion for speed, precision, and innovation. Our mission is to design, manufacture, and race high-performance IC engine go-karts, pushing the boundaries of technology and competing at the highest levels of collegiate motorsport.
